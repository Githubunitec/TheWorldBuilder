This comic is designed to be

F E L T



MEET ALTER EGO

In a world where the non-corporeal and the spiritual takes form, human souls fight the internal emotional battles of the real world. One such soul is Grievous Green - a young human soul who’s come out of five years of tormentous, non-stop bullying.

One night, mysteriously, his feelings of repressed anger, hatred and resentment take form, and turn into a creature that dubs himself Alter Ego. Alter Ego, incredibly intelligent yet bestial and violent, in the name of revenge, vows to destroy Green’s homeland along with everyone in it, and Green is now forced to chase him into increasingly dangerous territory to put a halt to his plans.

All the while, THE COMIC BOOK ITSELF tries to break the wall between you and the story with the goal of having you not only feel like Green - but BE Green. This is the essence of “FELT” - the almost sentient element of the comic book that continually will bend and break the comic book to have you not only experience the story, but BE there.
